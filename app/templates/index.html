About

RatWatch is a chatbot that lets you report real-time sightings and evidence in Atlanta through SMS. All you have to do is text RAT to 404-341-4471 and follow the steps. It's that easy! You can view the data gathered by RatWatch right here on this website. We have various statistics and a map for you to view the data in a simple and intuitive way. Feel free to take a look around!
